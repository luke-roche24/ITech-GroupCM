Privacy Policy About Us
PRIVACY POLICY
Data Collection

FitTrack collects only the information you provide: username, email (optional), and workout data.

Data Storage

Your password is encrypted using Django's built-in hashing. Your data is stored securely and only accessible to you.

Data Sharing

We do not sell or share your personal data. Friends can only see your workouts if you add them.

Your Rights

You can delete your account and all associated data at any time.
